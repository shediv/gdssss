Welcome to Goa Dhangar Samaj Seva Sangh, Sattari

Gdsss was founded in Dec 20, 1990 by a small group of uneducated but proactive social activist with aim of improving life style of brothers and sister of sattari dhangar samaj and to fight against social injustice hapenning to our people.

Since then this sangh has been working towards betterment of samaj in education, social life, cultural activities and political awarness. And today we stand strong with active commitee members and 531 members with alingned goals.

Education help

1. Providing free of cost scholarship forms and guidance to students for financial help

2. Awarding students every year passing in SSC, HSSC, Graduation and Post Graduacation

3. Special scholarship to topper in 12th science stream in the memory of Founder president Late Shri. Manohar Bhalu Doifode

4. Financial assistance to needy students

5. Handling scholariship form filling and submission process

Samaj Certificate

People from dhangar samaj can apply for this certificate by providing followings documents

1. Aadhar card

2. Birth Certificate showing residence proof on or before 1968

Certificates are given only to people born in dhangar samaj.

Area of focus

Sangh has always been focused on

1. Getting ST rights for dhangar samaj

2. Reducing unemployment

3. Awareness towards alcohol and tobacco free community

4. Guidance to young generation in social, sports and cultural fields

Urgents Matters

3. Samshan bhumi for dhangar bhandav
